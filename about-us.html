Who We Are

We are a group of 4 people, Abhishek, Aditya, Gaurish, Manthan, and together we are Hack 219.

All of us are in the first years of our bachelorate. Being new in the tech field, we started learning HTML CSS basics throughout the year participating in events and hackathons all along.

When we heard about Hackaccino, we knew we wanted to be here and give this idea a solid formation. We’re not a big corporation. We’re just four regular people who’ve poured our experiences, tips, and a whole lot of heart into this site — from hidden gems and must-see destinations to budget hacks and travel guides you can actually trust. Whether you're planning your first solo trip or a wild getaway with friends, we’re here to help you make the most of it.

Mission & Vision

To inspire, guide, and empower every traveler to explore the world with curiosity, confidence, and a sense of adventure. We believe that travel is more than just ticking off places on a map — it's about the memories you make, the people you meet, and the stories you collect along the way.

Through honest insights, helpful guides, and real experiences, we aim to make travel more accessible, meaningful, and fun for everyone — especially young explorers like us. Whether you're a spontaneous backpacker, a weekend getaway lover, or just someone dreaming of their next escape, whether you're on a budget or want a once in a lifetime full fledged VIP experience, we’re here to help you make it happen.
