Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel or modify my booking?
You can cancel or modify your booking by going to the "My Bookings" section and selecting the booking you wish to change. Click on the edit or cancel button and follow the instructions. Please note that cancellation policies vary depending on the package and timing.
What is your refund policy?
Our refund policy depends on when you cancel your booking. Cancellations made 30 days or more before the trip start date typically receive a full refund minus processing fees. Cancellations made 15-29 days before receive a 50% refund. Cancellations made less than 15 days before the trip may not be eligible for a refund.
How do I redeem my reward points?
You can redeem your reward points during the checkout process. When you're making a booking, you'll see an option to apply your available points toward the purchase. Each 100 points is equivalent to ₹100 off your booking.
What documents do I need for domestic travel?
For domestic travel within India, you typically need a government-issued photo ID such as an Aadhaar card, passport, driver's license, or voter ID card. For certain destinations or activities, additional permits may be required which will be specified in your booking details.
How can I get special accommodations for accessibility needs?
We strive to make travel accessible for everyone. Please contact our customer service team at least 72 hours before your trip to request special accommodations. We can arrange for wheelchair accessibility, rooms with accessibility features, and other special needs.
